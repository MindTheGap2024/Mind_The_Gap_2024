Mind the Gap 2024

Borders, Limits and Frontiers

Monday 20th May 2024

Online Conference


"Mind the Gap" is a one-day online conference that explores the complex nature

of borders in a global context. Speakers from various disciplines

will consider borders as dynamic constructs that are shaped by

policy, culture, and human interaction. Join us for a day

of insightful discussions on the evolving role matters

of security, identity and globalisation.
